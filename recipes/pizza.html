Spicy Meaty Pizza

This picture shows a golden thin Crust Spicy Meaty Pizza

This thin crust spicy pizza is for anyone who likes a good meaty pizza with a spicy kick. This recipe is easily adapatable as it is very easy to change the spicy kick or the meat on this pizza to whatever you desire. Making it perfect for any pizza lover.

Ingredients

  • 500g white bread flour or Tipo '00' flour
  • half a teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 7g dried yeast sachet
  • half a tablespoon golden caster sugar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Half a Chicken Breast cut into small pieces
  • 50g of Spicy Chorizo
  • 25g of Spicy Ground Beef
  • 2 Chillis of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons of chilli oil of your choice
  • 150g grated Mozzarella Cheese
  • 150g tomato puree
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves

Steps

  1. First make the pizza dough, follow the recipe on this link Click Here
  2. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C. Heat the chilli oil in a large frying pan over high heat. Add the chicken,chorizo and garlic and cook for 2 minutes
  3. Add the tomato puree to the pizza base you made earlier in step 1
  4. Spread out 2/3 of the mozzarella cheese over the pizza base
  5. Evenly spread out the chicken and Chorizo you cooked earlier onto the pizza. Followed by the remaining mozzarella
  6. Put the pizza in the preheated oven and cook for 12-15 minutes until the base is crispy and the mozzarella is golden
