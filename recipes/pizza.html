Spicy Meaty Pizza

Thin Crust Spicy Meaty Pizza

This thin crust spicy pizza is for anyone who likes a good meaty pizza with a spicy kick. This recipe is easily adapatable as it is very easy to change the spicy kick or the meat on this pizza to whatever you desire. Making it perfect for any pizza lover.

Ingredients

  • 500g white bread flour
  • half a teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 7g dried yeast sachet
  • half a tablespoon golden caster sugar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Half a Chicken Breast
  • 50g of Spicy Chorizo
  • 25g of Spicy Ground Beef
  • 2 Chillis of your choice
