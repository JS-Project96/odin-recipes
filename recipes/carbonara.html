Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

This picture shows a classic carbonara seasoned with salt + pepper

Discover how to make the perfect classic cheesy carbonara. An easy to follow recipe which will only take around 30 minutes from start to finish! This recipe will serve 4 people

Ingredients

  1. 100g Panchetta
  2. 50g Pecorino cheese
  3. 50g Parmesan cheese
  4. 3 Large eggs
  5. 350g Spaghetti
  6. 2 plump garlic cloves, peeled and left whole
  7. 50g unsalted butter
  8. sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
